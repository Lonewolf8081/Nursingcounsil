Florence Nightingale

(1820-1910)The Lady with the Lamp Florence Nightingale, known as the founder of modern nursing, was a pioneering nurse, statistician, and social reformer. Her efforts during the Crimean War significantly improved the standards of healthcare and hygiene in battlefield hospitals.

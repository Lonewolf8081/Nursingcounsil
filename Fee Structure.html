Fee Structure

The Authorities of all the Nursing Training Schools conducting the Nursing Courses of any category under the control of WEST BENGAL NURSING COUNCIL are hereby being informed that an enhancement has been made in fees of the following particulars and price of forms by the Members of the Council in the 174th General Body Meeting held on 18th June 2009 and it will be implemented with effect from 1st August 2009.

  • Sale of Form₹30

  • ANM(R), NUHM, NRHM & GNM₹420
  • Inservice Candidates (All Diploma Courses)₹1020

  • Mark-Sheet Fees₹130

  • Student Registration₹150
  • Final Registration (GNM)₹1050
  • Final Registration (ANM)₹550
  • Renewal of Registration₹400
  • Reciprocal Registration₹2050

  • Late Fees₹50
  • Penalty (every 3 months)₹50
  • Review of Answer Scripts₹2000
  • Scrutiny of Answer Scripts₹1000
  • Change of Surname₹550
  • Additional Qualifications (Per Course)₹1050
  • Abroad Verification₹6000
  • Document Verification₹1000
  • Old Document Search (>5 yrs)₹100
  • No Objection Certificate₹500

Refund Policy

Refunds will be returned to the original payment method and this goes for all pay modes from which the customer made the payment.

Cancellation Policy

Refund/Cancellation will not be entertained once the customer makes the payment.

Note: Fees revised on 204th General Body Meeting on 26/07/2024, w.e.f. 01/08/2024.
